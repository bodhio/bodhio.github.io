about

As a lover of philosophy and hip hop, I approach creating visual art like sampling music - referencing an existing work of art to create a new iteration with idiosyncratic style and flair. This process synthesizes the potential for artworks to be infinitely reimagined while individual pieces maintain a unique aura.

bio

I’ve been creating my whole life, as someone who is neurodivergent (diagnosed with ADHD) creating art is therapeutic. I currently work in the human-centered design field as a career, and continue to create art whenever I get the chance.

connect

As a creator I’m always open to connecting with others. Please do not hesitate to contact me if you are interested in collaborating on a project, commissioning some artwork, or just want to have a chat.

Carving out negative space, creating positive vibes.
